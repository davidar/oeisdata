## Maple
```Maple
ispal:= proc(n) local L;
  L:= convert(n,base,10);
  L = ListTools:-Reverse(L);
end proc:
R:= NULL: count:= 0: p:= 1:
for k from 1 while count < 11 do
  p:= nextprime(p);
  if ispal(k*p) then R:= R,k; count:= count+1 fi
od:
R; # _Robert Israel_, Feb 22 2023
```
