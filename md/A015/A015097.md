## Ruby
```Ruby
def A(q, n)
  ary = [1]
  (1..n).each{|i| ary << (0..i - 1).inject(0){|s, j| s + q ** j * ary[j] * ary[i - 1 - j]}}
  ary
end
def A015097(n)
  A(-2, n)
end # _Seiichi Manyama_, Dec 24 2016
```
