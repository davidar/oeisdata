## Maple
```Maple
s:= proc(i, j) option remember; `if`(i>j, 0, a(j)+s(i, j-1)) end:
a:= proc(n) option remember; local k; for k while
      ormap(isprime, [k+s(i, n-1)$i=1..n]) do od; k
    end:
seq(a(n), n=1..100);  # _Alois P. Heinz_, Mar 23 2020
```
