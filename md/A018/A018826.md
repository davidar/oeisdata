## Maple
```Maple
filter:= proc(n) local S,S2;
    S:= convert(convert(n,binary),string);
    S2:= convert(convert(n^2,binary),string);
    StringTools:-Search(S,S2)<>0
end proc:
select(filter, [$0..10000]); # _Robert Israel_, Jul 11 2018
```
