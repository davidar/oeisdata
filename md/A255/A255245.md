## Maple
```Maple
with(numtheory); P:=proc(q) local a,b,k,n;
for n from 2 to q do a:=sort([op(divisors(n))]);
b:=add(a[k]^2,k=1..nops(a)-1)/(nops(a)-1);
if type(b/n,integer) then lprint(n);
fi; od; end: P(10^6);
```
