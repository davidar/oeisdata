## Mathematica
```Mathematica
rev[n_]:=FromDigits[Reverse[IntegerDigits[n]]];radd[n_]:=n+rev[n];
```
```Mathematica
pal[n_]:=If[n==rev[n],True,False];
```
```Mathematica
raddN[n_]:=Length[NestWhileList[radd[#]&,n,pal[#]==False&]]-1;
```
