## Maple
```Maple
b:= proc(n, i) option remember; local f, g;
      if n=0 or i=1 then [1, 0]
    else f:= b(n, i-1); g:= `if`(i>n, [0$2], b(n-i, i));
         [f[1]+g[1], f[2]+g[2]+`if`(i=2, g[1], 0)]
      fi
    end:
a:= n-> b(n, n)[2]:
seq(a(n), n=1..50);  # _Alois P. Heinz_, May 18 2012
```
