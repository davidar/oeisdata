## Maple
```Maple
R := proc(n) local nn, nnn: nn:=convert(n, base, 10): add(nn[nops(nn)+1-j]*10^(j-1), j=1..nops(nn)): end:
A109910 := proc(n) return 10^length(max(R(n),1)) - R(n) - 1: end:
seq(A109910(n),n=0..70); # _Nathaniel Johnston_, Apr 28 2011
```
