## Mathematica
```Mathematica
AntiDivisors[n_] := Union[Drop[Drop[Divisors[2*n-1], 1], -1], Map[2*n/#&, Drop[Select[Divisors[2*n], OddQ], 1]], Drop[Drop[Divisors[2*n+1], 1], -1]]; Do[s = Plus @@ AntiDivisors[n]; If[IntegerQ[s^(1/3)], Print[n]], {n, 2, 10^5}]
```
