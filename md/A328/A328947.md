## Maple
```Maple
bintodec:= proc(n) local L,i; L:= convert(n,base,2); add(10^(i-1)*L[i],i=1..nops(L)) end proc:
select(t -> t mod 7 = 0, map(bintodec,[$0..1000]));
```
