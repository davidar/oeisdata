## Maple
```Maple
a:= proc(n) option remember; `if`(n<2, 4*n+1,
      (2*n+3)*a(n-1)-(n-1)^2*a(n-2))
    end:
seq(a(n), n=0..25);
# second Maple program:
a:= n-> n-> n! * add(binomial(n, i)*4^i/i!, i=0..n):
seq(a(n), n=0..25);
# third Maple program:
a:= n-> n!* simplify(LaguerreL(n, -4), 'LaguerreL'):
seq(a(n), n=0..25);
```
