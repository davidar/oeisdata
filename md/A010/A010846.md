## Maple
```Maple
A:= proc(n) local F, S, s,j,p;
  F:= numtheory:-factorset(n);
  S:= {1};
  for p in F do
    S:= {seq(seq(s*p^j, j=0..floor(log[p](n/s))),s=S)}
  od;
  nops(S)
end proc;
seq(A(n),n=1..1000); # _Robert Israel_, Jun 27 2014
```
