## Maple
```Maple
S:= {1}:
a[1]:= 1:
for n from 2 to 100 do
  t:=a[n-1]-(n+2);
  if t > 0 and not member(t,S) then
    a[n]:= t
  else
    a[n]:= a[n-1]+(n+2)
  fi;
  S:= S union {a[n]}
od:
seq(a[n],n=1..100); # _Robert Israel_, Feb 10 2017
```
