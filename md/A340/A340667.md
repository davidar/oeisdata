## Maple
```Maple
a:= n-> Bits[Join](subs(0=[0$n][], Bits[Split](n))):
seq(a(n), n=0..25);
# second Maple program:
b:= proc(n, k) option remember; `if`(n<2, n,
     `if`(irem(n, 2, 'r')=1, b(r, k)*2+1, b(r, k)*2^k))
    end:
a:= n-> b(n$2):
```
