## Maple
```Maple
b:= proc(u, o) option remember;
      `if`(u+o=0, 1, add(b(o-1+j, u-j), j=1..u))
    end:
a:= proc(n) a(n):=`if`(n=0, 1, a(n-1)*b(2*n-1, 0)) end:
seq(a(n), n=0..12);  # _Alois P. Heinz_, Mar 05 2021
```
