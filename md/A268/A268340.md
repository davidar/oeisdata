## Maple
```Maple
N:= 1000: # to get a(1)...a(N)
V:= Vector(N):
for p in select(isprime, [2,seq(i,i=3..isqrt(N),2)]) do
for k from 2 to floor(log[p](N)) do
  V[p^k]:= 1
od od:
convert(V,list); # _Robert Israel_, Dec 14 2017
```
