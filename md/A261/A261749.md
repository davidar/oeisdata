## Maple
```Maple
filter:= proc(n) local L1, L2;
  L1:= convert(n^2,base,10);
  L2:= convert((n+2)^2,base,10);
  evalb(sort(L1)=sort(L2));
end proc:
select(filter, [3*i+2 $ i = 1..10^5]); # _Robert Israel_, Aug 31 2015
```
