```Maple
b:= proc(n, i) option remember; `if`(n=0, {{}}, `if`(i<1, {},
      {b(n, i-1)[], seq(map(x->{x[], i}, b(n-i*j, i-1))[], j=1..n/i)}))
    end:
a:= n-> 2^n-nops(b(n$2)):
seq(a(n), n=0..33);  # _Alois P. Heinz_, Sep 04 2023
```
