## Maple
```Maple
sumodigs := proc(n) local dg; dg := convert(n,base,10) ; add(op(1+2*i,dg), i=0..floor(nops(dg)-1)/2) ; end proc:

A055017 := proc(n) sumodigs(n)-sumedigs(n) ; end proc: # _R. J. Mathar_, Aug 26 2011
```
