## Maple
```Maple
f:=proc(n) local t0,t1,i;
t0:=0; t1:=convert(n,base,10);
for i from 1 to nops(t1) do
if t1[i]>0 then t0:=t0+(10-t1[i])*10^(i-1); fi;
od:
RETURN(t0);
end;
```
