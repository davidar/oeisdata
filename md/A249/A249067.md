## Maple
```Maple
a:= proc(n) local j, s; s:={};
      for j while s<>{$1..9} do
        s:= s union {(p-> iquo(p, 10^(length(p)-1)))(j*n)}
      od; j-1
    end:
seq(a(n), n=1..100);  # _Alois P. Heinz_, Oct 20 2014
```
