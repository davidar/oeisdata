## Maple
```Maple
F:= combinat[fibonacci]:
b:= proc(n) local j;
      if n=0 then 0
    else for j from 2 while F(j+1)<=n do od;
         b(n-F(j))+2^(j-2)
      fi
    end:
a:= n-> 8*b(n-1)+3:
seq(a(n), n=1..60);  # _Alois P. Heinz_, Jun 17 2012
```
