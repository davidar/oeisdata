## Maple
```Maple
N:= 1000:  # to get a(n) for n <= N
R:= Vector(N):
for p from 2 to ilog2(N) do
  for i from 1 to floor(N^(1/p)) do
      R[i^p]:= 1
od od:
A069623:= map(round,Statistics:-CumulativeSum(R)):
convert(A069623,list); # _Robert Israel_, May 19 2014
```
