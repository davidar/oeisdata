## Maple
```Maple
b:= proc(n) option remember; local k; for k from
      1+`if`(n=1, 0, b(n-1)) while 0=mul(irem(i, 2),
      i=convert(k*(k+1)/2, base, 10) ) do od; k
    end:
a:= n-> (t-> t*(t+1)/2)(b(n)):
seq(a(n), n=1..50);  # _Alois P. Heinz_, Jul 12 2015
```
