## Maple
```Maple
a:= proc(n) local r, M, p, m; r, M, p, m:=
      <<1|0>, <0|1>>, <<0|1>, <1|1>>, n, n*(n+1);
      do if irem(p, 2, 'p')=1 then r:= r.M mod m fi;
         if p=0 then break fi; M:= M.M mod m
      od; r[1, 2]
    end:
seq(a(n), n=1..100);  # _Alois P. Heinz_, Nov 26 2016
```
