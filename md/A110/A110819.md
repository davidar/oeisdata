## Maple
```Maple
revdigs:= proc(n)
local L,nL,i;
L:= convert(n,base,10);
nL:= nops(L);
add(L[i]*10^(nL-i),i=1..nL);
end:
filter:= proc(n) local r;
  r:= revdigs(n);
  r <> n and numtheory:-factorset(r) = numtheory:-factorset(n)
end proc:
select(filter, [$10 .. 10^6]); # _Robert Israel_, Aug 14 2014
```
