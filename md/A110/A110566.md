## Maple
```Maple
H:= proc(n) H(n):= 1/n +`if`(n=1, 0, H(n-1)) end:
L:= proc(n) L(n):= ilcm(n, `if`(n=1, 1, L(n-1))) end:
a:= n-> L(n)/denom(H(n)):
seq(a(n), n=1..100);  # _Alois P. Heinz_, Aug 30 2012
```
