## Maple
```Maple
catn:= proc(a,b) 10^(1+ilog10(b))*a+b end proc:
R:= NULL: count:= 0:
for x from 2 by 2 while count < 100 do
  y:= catn(x^2,(x+1)^2);
  if isprime(y) then count:= count+1; R:= R, y; fi
od:
R; # _Robert Israel_, May 19 2020
```
