## Maple
```Maple
filter:= proc(n) local s,q,r;
  s:= convert(convert(n,base,10),`+`);
r:= n mod s;
  q:= (n-r)/s;
q+r = s^2
end proc:
select(filter, [$1..10^6]);
```
