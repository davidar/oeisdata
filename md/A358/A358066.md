## Python
```Python
terms = [1, 1]
for i in range(1,11):
    new_terms = []
    for j in range(1, i+1):
        for k in range(len(terms)):
            if terms[k] == j: new_terms.append(k+1)
    terms.extend(new_terms)
print(terms) # _Gleb Ivanov_, Nov 01 2022
```
