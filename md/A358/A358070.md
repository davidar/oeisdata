## Python
```Python
x=[{1},{1}]
for i in range(2,40):
    u=[]
    for j in range(1,i):
        u.extend([k*j//math.gcd(k,j) for k in x[i-j]])
    x.append(set(u))
xx=[set([i*j//math.gcd(i,j) for i in t for j in t]) for t in x]
print([max(i) for i in xx][2:])
```
