## Maple
```Maple
sopf:= proc(n) local t; add(t[1]*t[2],t=ifactors(n)[2]) end proc:
R:= NULL: count:= 0: T:= 0:
for n from 2 while count < 100 do
T:= T + sopf(n);
if isprime(T) then R:= R, n; count:= count+1 fi;
od:
R;
```
