## Python
```Python
def a(n):
  k = 1
  while k.bit_length() != (n+k).bit_length() or bin(k).count('1') != bin(n+k).count('1'): k += 1
  return k
print([a(n) for n in range(1, 62)]) # _Michael S. Branicky_, May 04 2021
```
