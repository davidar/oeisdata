## Python
```Python
MAX=10000
dp=[False]*(MAX+2)
an=0
dp[an]=True
while an<MAX:
  print(an)
  while dp[an]:
    an+=1
  an+=1
  while dp[an]:
    an+=1
  for i in reversed(range(an,MAX)):
    dp[i]|=dp[i-an]
```
