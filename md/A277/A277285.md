```Maple
a:= proc(n) option remember; local k;
      for k from 1+`if`(n=1, 0, a(n-1))
      while 0<>2&^k-1 mod numtheory[tau](k) do od; k
    end:
seq(a(n), n=1..50);  # _Alois P. Heinz_, Nov 06 2016
```
