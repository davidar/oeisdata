## Maple
```Maple
ds:= proc(x) local t,s,r;
  s:= x; t:= 0;
  while s > 0 do
    r:= s mod 10;
    t:= t + r;
    s:= (s-r)/10;
  od;
t
end proc:
seq(add(ds(x^2),x=10^(n-1)..10^n-1), n=1..5);
```
