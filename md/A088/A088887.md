## Maple
```Maple
b:= proc(n, i) option remember; `if`(n=0, {[]}, `if`(i<1, {},
      {b(n, i-1)[], seq(map(x->sort([x[], j]),
                    b(n-i*j, i-1))[], j=1..n/i)}))
    end:
a:= n-> nops(b(n, n)):
seq(a(n), n=0..50);  # _Alois P. Heinz_, Feb 19 2013
```
