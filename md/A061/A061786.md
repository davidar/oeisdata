## Maple
```Maple
b:= proc(n) b(n):= {seq(n^2+i^2, i=1..n)} end:
s:= proc(n) s(n):= `if`(n=0, {}, b(n) union s(n-1)) end:
a:= n-> nops(s(n)):
seq(a(n), n=1..100);  # _Alois P. Heinz_, May 07 2014
```
