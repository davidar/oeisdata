## Maple
```Maple
N:= 500: # to get terms <= N
L:= map(t -> max(convert(t,base,10)), [$1..N+1]):
LL:= L[1..-2]+L[2..-1]:
select(t -> member(LL[t], {2, 3, 5, 7, 11, 13, 17}), [$1..N]); # _Robert Israel_, May 20 2020
```
