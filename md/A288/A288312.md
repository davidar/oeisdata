## Maple
```Maple
b:= proc(n, k) option remember; `if`(k=n, n!,
      `if`(k=0, 0, n*(b(n-1, k-1)+b(n-1, k)*k/(n-k))))
    end:
a:= n-> b(2*n, n):
seq(a(n), n=0..15);
```
