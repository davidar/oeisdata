## Maple
```Maple
f:= proc(n) local x,y;
  for x from 2 to 10 do
    y:= x^n;
    if floor(y/10^ilog10(y)) = 1 then return x^n fi
  od
end proc:
f(1):= 1:
map(f, [$1..50]); # _Robert Israel_, Aug 13 2019
```
