## Maple
```Maple
N:= 1000: # to get terms up to a(N)
B:= Array(1..N, numtheory:-issqrfree):
C:= map(`if`,B,0,1):
A:= map(round,Statistics:-CumulativeSum(C)):
seq(A[n],n=1..N); # _Robert Israel_, Jun 03 2014
```
