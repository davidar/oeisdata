## Maple
```Maple
N:= 1000: # to get all terms <= N
Primes:= select(isprime, {2,seq(i,i=3..floor(N/4))}):
S:= select(`<=`,{seq(p^5, p = Primes),seq(seq(p*q^2, p=Primes minus {q}),q=Primes)},N):
sort(convert(S,list)); # _Robert Israel_, Feb 10 2016
```
