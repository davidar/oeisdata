## Maple
```Maple
a[1]:= 1:
for n from 2 to 100 do
a[n]:= n*10^(1+ilog10(a[n-1])) + a[n-1]
od:
seq(a[n],n=1..100); # _Robert Israel_, Sep 05 2014
```
