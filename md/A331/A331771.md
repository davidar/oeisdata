## Maple
```Maple
VR := proc(m,n,q) local a,i,j; a:=0;
for i from -m+1 to m-1 do for j from -n+1 to n-1 do
if gcd(i,j)=q then a:=a+(m-abs(i))*(n-abs(j)); fi; od: od: a; end;
[seq(VR(n,n,1),n=1..50)];
```
