## Haskell
```Haskell
a001157 n = s n 1 1 a000040_list where
   s 1 1 y _          = y
   s m x y ps'@(p:ps)
     | m `mod` p == 0 = s (m `div` p) (x * p^2) y ps'
     | x > 1          = s m 1 (y * (x * p^2 - 1) `div` (p^2 - 1)) ps
     | otherwise      = s m 1 y ps
-- _Reinhard Zumkeller_, Jul 10 2011
```
