```Mathematica
PrimeDivisor[n_]:=Part[Transpose[FactorInteger[n]],1]
```
```Mathematica
Omega[n_]:=If[n==1,0,Sum[IntegerExponent[n,Part[PrimeDivisor[n],i]],{i,1,Length[PrimeDivisor[n]]}]]
```
