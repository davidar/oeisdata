```Maple
f:= proc(n) option remember; local d;
d:= n mod 10;
d + (-1)^d + 10*procname((n-d)/10)
end proc:
for i from 0 to 9 do f(i):= i + (-1)^i od:
map(f, [$0..100]); # _Robert Israel_, Feb 20 2019
```
