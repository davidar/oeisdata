## Maple
```Maple
filter:= proc(n) local L,h;
  L:= map(t->1/t,numtheory:-divisors(n));
  denom(nops(L)/convert(L,`+`))=2;
end proc:
select(filter, [$1..10^6]); # _Robert Israel_, Oct 17 2021
```
