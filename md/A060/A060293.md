## Python
```Python
from math import ceil
n=100 #number of terms
ans=0
finalans = [0]
for i in range(1, n+1):
    ans+=(1/i)
    finalans.append(ceil(ans*i))
print(finalans)
# _Adam Hugill_, Feb 14 2022
```
