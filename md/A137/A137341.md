## Maple
```Maple
b:= proc(n) option remember; `if`(n=0, 1,
      add(b(n-j)*binomial(n-1, j-1), j=1..n))
    end:
a:= n-> b(n)*n!:
seq(a(n), n=0..23);  # _Alois P. Heinz_, Aug 30 2019
```
