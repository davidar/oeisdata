## Maple
```Maple
revdigs:= proc(n)
local L,nL,i;
L:= convert(n,base,10);
nL:= nops(L);
add(L[i]*10^(nL-i),i=1..nL);
end:
Primes:= select(isprime,{2,seq(2*i+1,i=1..5*10^5)}):
Primes intersect map(revdigs,Primes); # _Robert Israel_, Aug 14 2014
```
