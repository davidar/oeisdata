## Maple
```Maple
a := proc(n) local u,b,w,k;
u := 1; w := 1; b := true;
for k from 2 to n do
   u := u + 2;
   w := u + `if`(b, -w, +w);
   b := not b;
od; w end:
seq(a(n), n=1..95); # _Peter Luschny_, Dec 31 2014
```
