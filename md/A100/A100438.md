```Maple
f:=proc(n) local i,j,k,l,t1; t1:={}; for i from 1 to n-3 do for j from i+1 to n-2 do for k from j+1 to n-1 do for l from k+1 to n do t1:={op(t1),i*j*k*l}; od: od: od: od: t1:=convert(t1,list); nops(t1); end;
```
