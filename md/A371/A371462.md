## Maple
```Maple
filter:= proc(x) local F,n,mu,i;
  F:= convert(x,base,10);
  n:= nops(F);
  mu:= convert(F,`+`)/n;
  evalb(2*mu^2 = add(F[i]^2,i=1..n)/n)
end proc:
select(filter, [$0..10000]); # _Robert Israel_, Mar 24 2024
```
