## Maple
```Maple
b:= proc(n, i, m) option remember; `if`(n=0, m, `if`(i=1, min(m, n),
      add(b(n-i*j, i-1, `if`(j=0, m, min(m, j))), j=0..n/i)))
    end:
a:= n-> b(n$3):
seq(a(n), n=1..50);  # _Alois P. Heinz_, Sep 17 2023
```
